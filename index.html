Welcome to Cherville Bed & Breakfast

Located within walking distance of Cavan town we are close to everything you may need. We are just minutes away from Wedding venues such as the Radisson Blu Farnham Estate, Cavan Crystal and Hotel Kilmore. We are also very close to the Cavan Equestrian Centre and Cavan Genealogy Centre.

Our main priority is the comfort of our guests, we go out of our way to provide exceptional service and ensure that everybody leaves with a smile on their face.
